ABOUT ME

Mechanical Engineering Background with Computer Science Aspirations...

Are you ready for the elevator pitch? My name is Mystery Man, and I am currently a Project Engineer, with a breadth of experience. I started out as a Mechanical Designer, assisting in designing and helping run the mechanical design team responsible for equipment in use by millions of people per day. I have since moved up to a position where I now am responsible for the entirety of technical aspects of a similar project in a neighboring city. I spend time now working through large codebases, debugging api errors, configuring and understanding large databases. I provide customer facing documentation that is meant to clearly convey the design and intent of the products being provided under contract.

But... That is what I do and did, not what I want to do moving forward. I have always had a passion for Computer Science and Software Engineering, but only really recently started exploring it again. I took some of my first electives in CS (C and java was an interesting start...) and have since continued my pursuit. I am currently pursuing a Masters in Computer Science through Baylor University. It has been a challenge with my current role, but it is worth it and immensely satisfying.
